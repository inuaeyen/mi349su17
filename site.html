About us:

Agritec Holdings Ltd is a holding company incorporating three other companies. Obong Bassey Inuaeyen is the the Group Executive Chairman, in charge of coordination of the operations of all the companies under the group. Mrs. Ifeoma Inuaeyen is the Executive Director and second in command of the companies. Our office, as shown above, is located in G.R.A. Ikeja, Lagos. Our main company deals with the import and sale of brand name generators and converters. While the other two companies are into the sale of Household Equipments and offering of Submersible Pumps to home owners respectively.
